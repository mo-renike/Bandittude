About Bandittude

Bandittude is an eclectic all female live band exploring the Afro, Highlife and Funk inspired genres of music. The band was formed in September 2019 by Morenike Oyewole to create a platform that allows, inspires and encourages Women to pursue, explore and achieve their potential to the fullest. Bandittude aims to inspire women and girls around the world as they contribute to the Girl Power movement, using music as a tool..


The band’s sound is mostly original instrumental arrangements and songs composed by the music director Nonso Godwin who is a Composer, singer and performer. The band also performs as a backup band for other artists. They draw inspiration from the work of artists/bands like The Compozers, Alternate sound, Kool and the gang and The hooligans.


Within a few months since inception, The band has staged Valerie concert in collaboration with Freedom Park Lagos for the 2019 International Day of the girl child, performeed at Felabration, at the “Celebration of Amazons” event at the Ikoyi Club, the “I Am Amazon” concert, the 2020 International Women’s day event by Ladies in Sports International, Ladies’ Night organized by Freedom park among others.


They are currently working on releasing an EP titled “Emergence”. They also record “Band Attack Sessions” where they remix, rearrange and reharmonize popular songs. Bandittude is known for Original Arrangements, Covers and Repertoire. They are also known for keeping the audience amazed on it’s feet at concerts and other events..

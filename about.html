About Bandittude

Bandittude is an eclectic all female live band exploring the Afro, Highlife and Funk inspired genres of music. The band was formed in September 2019 by Morenike Oyewole to create a platform that allows, inspires and encourages Women to pursue, explore and achieve their potential to the fullest. Bandittude aims to inspire women and girls around the world as they contribute to the Girl Power movement, using music as a tool.


Our sound is mostly original instrumental arrangements and songs. We also perform as a backup band for other artists.

We are currently working on releasing an EP while recording “Band Attack Sessions” where we remix, rearrange and reharmonize popular songs. Bandittude is known for Original Arrangements, Covers and Repertoire. They are also known for keeping the audience amazed on it’s feet at concerts and other events.
